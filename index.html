Codebrowser?

Codebrowser helps researchers (and others!) to visualize and tag programming code snapshots taken by various IDEs. As the amount of storage formats varies with every IDE and snapshot gathering tool, Codebrowser provides a simple REST API that different storage formats can be integrated to.

Some samples for building your own backend already exist; if you'd like to have help integrating your snapshot gathering mechanism to Codebrowser, send an issue on github and let us know about your dataformat -- or even better, send a pull-request or a link, and we'll link your project to our site!

I'm not allowed to share my data!

Exactly the reason why codebrowser was built! Codebrowser acts as an UI for your data, giving you the opportunity to collaborate with those you choose to collaborate with -- codebrowser does not know anything about your data, and will not gather any information about your data.
